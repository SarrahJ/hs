Welcome to Human Skills

Our Human Skills programme is designed to increase the effectiveness and capacity of teams. It builds the resourcefulness, adaptability and self awareness of each individual within your team. Expect the best out of your people, and help them to flourish. If your team is ready for change, we want to work with you. Get in touch to discuss your needs.

Why Human Skills?

Skills based interpersonal training for your team

Research by the Harvard “Aristotle" project tells us that high performing teams need psychological safety to thrive. This is created when everyone has the chance to voice their thoughts to the group without fear of judgement or rejection. The unique and highly effective facilitation style of this programme will enable your team to go on a journey together that not only brings you closer, but strengthens bonds to be the best team you can be.

We have built this programme through Enspiral Dev Avademy and have successfully graduated over 400 students with these skills. Our hope is that our Dev Academy students graduate into a caring workforce that continues to encourage their growth.

Outcomes

Get on the same page

Participants in the Human Skills programme gain shared skills and a common language to take back to their day-to-day interactions. This assists you in resolving conflict, managing difficult conversations and being aware of each other’s needs. We support you every step of the way to integrate what you learn.

Increase Effectiveness and Capacity

Through our sessions, individuals learn self reflection and emotional regulation skills that make it easier for teams to work together. As capacity for work builds so does the enjoyment and wellbeing outcomes for all staff.

Teamwork and Communication Skills

At Enspiral Dev Academy we believe the power of kind and productive communication is crucial to the workplaces of today, and tomorrow. Minimise conflict and learn how to build trust.

Our Offerings

Bootcamp Experience

This is the consultation, preparation and delivery of Human Skills over eight separate sessions. This is the entire eight week curriculum delivered in the manner of a Dev Academy programme.

We take your team on a journey for 8 weeks that includes:

  • 8 x 90 min facilitated sessions (teams of 8-15 people)
  • 8 x 15-20min practice/study group sessions to be held over video conference
  • 5 hours consulting time over two months on leadership, rhythms and cultural practices
  • Workbooks and learning materials
  • Resources
  • Follow up session in 6 months
The content of the workshops can be tailored to suit your needs.

Workshop Series

This programme covers the consultation, preparation and delivery of Human Skills over four separate sessions.

This includes:

  • 4 x 90min facilitated sessions
  • 2 hours of consulting time before and during the sessions
  • Workbooks and learning materials
  • Resources
  • Follow up session in 6 months
These workshops can be tailored to meet your needs and are available as single workshops to boost team morale or as a great addition to your next team building event.
